💡 Lutron RadioRA 3 — Whole-Home Lighting

Wireless · Keypads · Fully integrated
🧠 How It Operates

Lutron RA3 controls every light in the main living areas wirelessly — no wiring back to switches. Your electrician runs the fixtures hot (one continuous circuit, up to 50 bulbs), and we handle all the low-voltage keypad wiring. That means significant savings on electrical labor — typically 30% less than traditional wiring. Keypads in main areas replace the wall of switches with one clean panel. You can also control everything by voice through Josh or remotely from the app.
