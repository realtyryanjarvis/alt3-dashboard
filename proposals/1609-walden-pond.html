A Day at 1609 Walden Pond
Your home,
working for you.
Here's what a real day looks like once your home is intelligent.
🌅
Morning

The house starts before you do

Lights ease on gradually in the living area and kitchen. The thermostat shifts to daytime. Soft music plays in the kitchen. You walk in, and the 10" touchscreen shows you who's outside on camera — all before you've touched your phone.

🥂
Entertaining

One tap. The whole home shifts.

Guests are coming. Tap the keypad once — or just say "entertaining." Lights warm throughout the open space, music fills the kitchen, dining area, and living room, outdoor speakers come on by the pool and outdoor kitchen, and the landscape lights activate. Every zone, together, at once.

📺
Movie Night

The room transforms in seconds

Say "movie time." The TV lowers to the perfect viewing angle. Surround sound activates — Arc in front, speakers in the walls for the full effect. Lights go dark. Shades close. HVAC quiets down. All of it, at once, without touching a single remote.

🛏️
Bedtime

The whole house shuts down from bed

In-ceiling speakers in the master bedroom play the TV sound overhead — quiet and clear, no soundbar blasting. Say "goodnight" and every light in the house goes off, security arms, doors lock, thermostat drops to sleep mode. You don't get up. It just happens.

🚗
Away

Leave without second-guessing anything

Say "heading out" at the door. Everything off. Security armed. Thermostat to away. You're already in the car and not wondering if you left something on — because you know you didn't. And if you want to check, the app shows you live cameras from anywhere.
