Voice Intelligence
Meet Your Home's Intelligence Layer
Josh.ai understands natural language. No keywords, no programmed routines. Just speak naturally — your home responds.
Good morning
→
Shades open to your preferred level. Lights come up to energizing daylight tones. Coffee schedule activates. HVAC shifts to day mode.
Movie time in the bonus room
→
Shades close. Lights dim to cinematic levels. Anthem receiver powers on. Surround speakers activate. TV switches input automatically.
Play something in the kitchen
→
Your preferred music service picks up in the kitchen zone — independent of whatever's playing elsewhere in the house.
Goodnight
→
Every light off. Doors locked. Security armed. Thermostat to sleep mode. One word — the whole house responds.
