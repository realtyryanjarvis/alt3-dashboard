How It Works
We Fit Into Your Build Process Seamlessly
Five touchpoints across your build timeline — everything else is handled by us.
1
Discovery
We review your plans, discuss the buyer profile, and spec the right package for the price point.
2
Prewire Spec
Before framing, we deliver a complete prewire plan to your electrician. No guesswork, no rework.
3
Rough-In
We coordinate with your subs during rough-in to confirm conduit, low-voltage, and rack locations.
4
Trim & Install
Fixtures, keypads, speakers, touchscreens, cameras — all installed and commissioned at trim stage.
5
Buyer Handoff
We walk the buyer through their system at closing. They leave knowing exactly how to use everything.
