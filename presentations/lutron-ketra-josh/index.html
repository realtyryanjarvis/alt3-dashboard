System 01 — Lighting & Shading
Lutron RadioRA 3 —
Control Everything.

RadioRA 3 is Lutron's flagship whole-home wireless system. No new wiring. Engineered to work flawlessly for decades. The system installers trust when there is no margin for error.

What Lutron Controls
  • Every light in every room — dimmers, switches, receptacles on one unified system
  • Motorized shades & drapery — blackout, solar, and sheer layers with one tap or command
  • HVAC via thermostat integration — temperature follows occupancy and time of day
  • Outdoor lighting — landscape, driveway, security, and accent, all scene-controlled
  • Whole-home scenes — one tap or one voice command sets the entire house
  • Geofencing — lights on when you arrive, off when you leave, automatically
Why Lutron Beats Everything Else
  • Clear Connect RF — Lutron's proprietary protocol is 10× more reliable than Z-Wave or Zigbee
  • Sub-100ms response — tap to light with no perceptible delay, ever
  • Works without internet — full local control always, no cloud dependency
  • Native Ketra integration — Ketra is a Lutron company; they communicate at protocol level
  • No hub required — the RA3 main repeater is the hub; nothing to add later
  • Backward compatible — works with Caseta, RA2, and HW devices already in the home
