System 01 · Lighting & Shading Control

Lutron
RadioRA 3

The most reliable, most spec'd residential lighting control system in the world. If your home has Lutron, it has the best.

Lutron RadioRA 3

Control Every Light.
From Anywhere. Every Time.

RadioRA 3 is Lutron's flagship whole-home wireless system — no additional wiring, fully backward-compatible, and engineered to work flawlessly for decades. It's the system installers trust when there's no margin for error.

What Lutron Controls in Your Home

  • Every light in every room — dimmers, switches, and receptacles on one unified system
  • Motorized shades and drapery (Lutron Sivoia QS or Palladiom) — blackout, solar, and sheer layers
  • HVAC integration via thermostat — temperature follows occupancy and time-of-day programming
  • Outdoor lighting — landscape, driveway, security, and accent, all scene-controlled
  • Whole-home scenes: one tap or one command sets the entire house
  • Geofencing: lights on when you arrive, off when you leave — automatically

Why Lutron Beats Everything Else

  • Clear Connect RF — Lutron's proprietary RF protocol is 10× more reliable than Z-Wave or Zigbee
  • No hub required — the RA3 main repeater IS the hub; nothing to add or update
  • Fastest response time — sub-100ms from tap to light. No perceptible delay, ever
  • Backward compatible — works with Caseta, RA2, and HW devices already in the home
  • Native Ketra integration — Ketra is a Lutron company; they communicate at protocol level
  • Works without internet — local control always. No cloud dependency

System 02 · Tunable Natural Light

Ketra
Natural Light

Light is the most powerful design element in a home — and most homes get it completely wrong. Ketra fixes that.

Ketra by Lutron

Light That Moves
With the Day.

Ketra is the only residential lighting system that replicates the full color and intensity arc of natural daylight — from the warm amber of morning, through the crisp bright white of midday, to the soft amber glow of evening. It's not just beautiful. It changes how you feel, sleep, and live in the space.

What Makes Ketra Different

  • Full-spectrum tunable white — 1400K to 10000K color temperature range in a single fixture
  • DayLight Management — automatically shifts color temp and intensity to match the sun's position throughout the day
  • Full color capability — any color on the spectrum, not just white. Perfect for accent, art, and entertainment spaces
  • Vibrant color saturation — 90+ CRI ensures colors in your home look exactly right under Ketra light
  • Lutron-native — Ketra is a Lutron brand; zero compatibility issues, single app, single control system
  • No visible flicker — engineered to exceed medical-grade standards for flicker-free operation

The Science of DayLight Management

Morning — 6:00–10:00am

Warm amber (~2200K) · Low intensity · Gentle on eyes · Supports natural cortisol wake cycle

Midday — 10:00am–3:00pm

Crisp daylight white (~5500K) · Full brightness · Maximum focus and productivity

Evening — 5:00–10:00pm

Warm candlelight (~2700K) · Dims naturally · Triggers melatonin, supports sleep quality
