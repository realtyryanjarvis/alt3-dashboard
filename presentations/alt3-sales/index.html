Flagship System

Homeworks
QSX

Large-Scale & Estate Homes

The pinnacle of Lutron engineering. Homeworks QSX is a processor-based, enterprise-grade lighting control platform built for homes where precision, scalability, and longevity are non-negotiable.

  • Capacity: Unlimited zones — scales from a single room to a 30,000 SF estate
  • Processors: Dedicated HW-QSX processor + optional redundant backup processor
  • Keypads: Palladiom designer keypads — machined aluminum, fully customizable engraving
  • Integration: Native Josh.ai, Control4, Crestron, Savant, HVAC, shades, AV
  • Shades: Full Sivoia QS & Palladiom motorized shade integration
  • Ketra: Native Ketra tunable white + full-spectrum color support
  • Installation: Requires certified Lutron dealer (Alt3 is Lutron Diamond partner)
  • Best for: New construction, major renovation, $3M+ homes, multi-structure estates
Accessible System

RadioRA
3

Mid-Scale & Retrofit

RadioRA 3 brings Lutron's legendary reliability to homes that need smart lighting without a full processor infrastructure. Wireless-first, with all the Lutron quality you expect.

  • Capacity: Up to 200 devices, 100 zones — ideal for most luxury single-family homes
  • Architecture: Wireless RF mesh — no dedicated wiring runs required
  • Keypads: Sunnata designer keypads — premium feel, multiple finish options
  • Integration: Josh.ai, Apple HomeKit, Amazon Alexa, Google Home, smart home bridges
  • Shades: Serena motorized shades — wireless, battery or hardwired
  • Ketra: Compatible with select Ketra fixtures via ecosystem bridge
  • Installation: Faster install — no low-voltage wiring infrastructure required
  • Best for: Retrofit projects, $1M–$3M homes, additions, phased installs

You're building or doing a full renovation

New construction, major gut renovation, estate homes over 5,000 SF, multi-structure properties, or any project where walls are open and infrastructure can be run properly. QSX is built to last decades and scale as your home evolves.

Retrofit projects, phased upgrades, or homes where pulling wire isn't practical. RadioRA 3 gives you Lutron reliability and quality with a faster, less invasive installation — still integrates beautifully with Josh.ai and Ketra.
